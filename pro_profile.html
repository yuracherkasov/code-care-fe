MAIN IDEA

In 2014 Sigma Technology launched a new market concept with the campaign “We are technology.” The idea behind it was to strengthen the company’s position on the market in the area of technology, boost employees’ motivation, attract new talents to the company and spread the corporate culture externally.

The decision to launch a dedicated web section with employees’ stories was originated from the ambition to promote people, working at Sigma, make technology personal and something that the reader, him or herself, can relate to.

Sigma Software performed wed design and web development, turning the initial concept into a smart web presence.
